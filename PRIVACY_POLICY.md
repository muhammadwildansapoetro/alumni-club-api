# Privacy Policy for FTIP Alumni Club

**Effective Date:** December 14, 2025

## 1. Introduction

The FTIP Alumni Club ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal information collected through our alumni management platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Email Address:** Used as your unique identifier and for communication
- **Full Name:** Your complete name for alumni directory and identification
- **Google Account Information:** When you authenticate via Google OAuth, we receive your Google ID and basic profile information

### 2.2 Alumni Profile Information
We collect detailed alumni information including:
- **Academic Information:** Department (TEP/TPN/TIN), class year
- **Location:** City of residence
- **Professional Information:**
  - Industry field (Agriculture, Food Tech, Biotech, Research, Education, Engineering, Business, Marketing, Finance, Government, Freelance, Other)
  - Employment level (Intern, Staff, Supervisor, Manager, Senior Manager, Director, VP, C-Level, Founder, Other)
  - Income range (Below 5M, 5-10M, 10-20M, Above 20M IDR, Unknown)
  - Job title and company name
- **Professional Social Media:** LinkedIn profile URL (optional)

### 2.3 User-Generated Content
If you choose to contribute, we collect:
- **Job Postings:** Title, description, company, location, job type, salary range, external URLs
- **Business Directory Listings:** Business name, description, category, location, website, contact information

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information
- **Authentication Data:** Google OAuth tokens and authentication status
- **Usage Data:** System interactions and API access patterns
- **Technical Data:** IP addresses, device information, browser type (for security purposes)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Authentication & Account Management:** To verify your identity and manage your account
- **Alumni Directory:** To maintain a searchable directory of FTIP alumni
- **Professional Networking:** To facilitate connections between alumni
- **Career Opportunities:** To share job postings and business opportunities
- **Statistical Analysis:** To generate aggregate statistics about FTIP alumni (anonymized data)

### 3.2 Specific Uses
- **Email Communication:** Account notifications, system updates, and relevant alumni news
- **Profile Management:** Allowing you to update and maintain your alumni profile
- **Content Moderation:** Reviewing user-generated job postings and business listings
- **Security:** Protecting against unauthorized access and fraudulent activities

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
- All data is stored securely on Supabase's PostgreSQL cloud database
- Database is hosted in AWS infrastructure with enterprise-grade security

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Authentication:** Secure Google OAuth 2.0 integration with token-based authentication
- **Access Control:** Role-based access control (User/Admin roles)
- **Rate Limiting:** API endpoints protected against abuse with rate limiting
- **Soft Deletion:** User data is marked as deleted rather than permanently removed for data integrity

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained as long as your account remains active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is soft-deleted and retained for system integrity
- **Statistical Data:** Aggregated statistics may be retained indefinitely

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Public Information
The following information is visible to other authenticated alumni:
- **Basic Profile:** Name, department, class year, city
- **Professional Information:** Industry, employment level, job title, company (if provided)
- **LinkedIn Profile:** If you choose to include it

### 5.2 Private Information
The following information is never shared publicly:
- Email addresses (visible only to administrators)
- Google authentication tokens
- Income range information (used only for aggregate statistics)
- Account deletion status

### 5.3 Third-Party Services
- **Google OAuth:** We use Google's authentication service for secure login
- **Supabase:** Database hosting and management
- **AWS Cloud Infrastructure:** Server hosting and data storage

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Data Access and Control
- **View:** You can view all your personal data through your profile
- **Update:** You can update your alumni profile information at any time
- **Delete:** You can request account deletion (soft deletion with data retention)
- **Export:** You can request a copy of your personal data

### 6.2 Authentication Choices
- **Google OAuth:** We use Google as our exclusive authentication provider
- **Account Security:** You control access through your Google account

### 6.3 Communication Preferences
- **Email Notifications:** Essential account communications cannot be disabled
- **Promotional Communications:** You can opt-out of non-essential communications

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

### 7.1 Authentication Cookies
- **JWT Tokens:** Secure authentication tokens with 7-day expiration
- **Session Management:** Cookies to maintain your logged-in status

### 7.2 Security Cookies
- **CSRF Protection:** Security tokens to prevent cross-site request forgery
- **Rate Limiting:** Cookies to enforce API usage limits

## 8. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for adult alumni of FTIP Unpad. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located outside of Indonesia. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

## 10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:
- Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
- Provide details about the breach and its impact
- Outline steps taken to mitigate the breach

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via:
- Email notifications
- Website notifications
- In-app announcements

## 13. Legal Basis for Processing

Under applicable data protection laws, we process your personal data based on:
- **Legitimate Interest:** Maintaining the alumni network and facilitating professional connections
- **Contractual Necessity:** Providing the alumni services you have requested
- **Consent:** Where you have explicitly consented to specific processing activities

## 14. Specific Data Processing Details

### 14.1 Statistical Data Processing
- Personal data used for statistics is aggregated and anonymized
- No individual can be identified from published statistics
- Income data is only used in aggregated form for demographic analysis

### 14.2 Job Posting and Business Directory
- User-generated content is moderated but not edited
- Contact information in business listings is public to other alumni
- Job postings may include external links to third-party websites

### 15. Technical Implementation Details

### 15.1 API Security
- All API endpoints require authentication
- Password-based authentication has been discontinued in favor of secure Google OAuth
- Rate limiting prevents automated abuse (100 requests/15 minutes general, 15 requests/5 minutes for authentication)

### 15.2 Data Integrity
- Database transactions ensure data consistency
- Regular backups protect against data loss
- Audit logs track administrative actions
